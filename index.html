AI Ping-Pong Game

Instructions for how to play the game:

  1. First keep your device screen straight and move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

  2. Move your right wrist up and down to control your paddle, a purple dot should appear on your right wrist

  3. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from your screen.

  4. Keep your eye on the ball and have fun!
  5. P.S. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
